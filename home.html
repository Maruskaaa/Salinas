About

Experience the ultimate surf getaway at our newly rebuilt flat just a few minutes' walk from the famous Salinas beach. This one-bedroom oasis is perfect for up to four people, whether you're a group of friends or a family seeking adventure and relaxation. With a private garage, you'll have peace of mind knowing your vehicle is secure.

Designed with digital nomads in mind, our flat is equipped with all the amenities you need to stay connected while enjoying the beach lifestyle. Work comfortably from our dedicated workspace, complete with high-speed internet and a comfortable desk. Unwind after a productive day with a refreshing dip in the ocean or take a stroll along the sandy shores.

Immerse yourself in the vibrant surf culture of Salinas, renowned for its excellent waves and thrilling water sports. Whether you're a seasoned surfer or a beginner eager to learn, this is the perfect destination. And when you're not riding the waves, explore the local shops, restaurants, and entertainment options just moments away.
